Clear Space & Minimum Size
Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to at least half the height of the crest. The minimum width for print is 25mm and for digital is 120px.
Incorrect Usage
Do not stretch, rotate, change colours, add effects, or place the logo on busy backgrounds. Never recreate the logo â€” always use the approved files.

Typography
Primary Typeface (Digital & Print): Arial or system sans-serif for excellent readability across all platforms.
Accent / Display: A strong, confident sans-serif may be used for headlines and the strapline.
Visual Style & Photography
Photography should feel warm, authentic, and appetising. Favour natural lighting, rich colours, and genuine moments (food being cooked low and slow, people enjoying BBQ together, pitmasters at work). Avoid overly staged or clinical food photography.
Social Media & Digital Applications
Use the Primary Crest as the main profile picture. The Gold version works well for premium or dark-themed posts. Always maintain good contrast and never stretch the logo.
